Overview

Purpose

Allow users to compare, filter, and explore through a broad range of PC's. They will be able to look at how certain parts will perform in certain games for the cost. If they choose, they may contact us through a form for a custom build or more information.

Audience

This site is intended for anyone who is looking to build their own pc, but wants to know how specific parts and configurations would work in their favorite game.

Dynamic elements

The main page will have a search and or filter/comparison boxes, to compare based on games, performance, and parts. The child page will contain a form allowing users to contact us with questions or inqueries about custom builds.
